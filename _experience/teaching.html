I have TA'ed the following courses while studying at University of Toronto:
  • CSCA08 Introduction to Computer Science (in Python)
  • CSCA48 Introduction to Computer Science (in C)
  • CSCB09 Software Tools and Systems Programming (shell scripting and C)
  • CSCC85 Fundamentals of Robotics and Automated Systems
  • CSCD27 Computer and Network Security
My official duties included:
  • Leading tutorials and office hours
  • Grading quizzes, assignments and exams
  • Updating assignments/labs
  • Updating/rewriting auto-markers
  • Answering forum questions
  • Invigilating tests and exams
In addition, to my official duties, I created and maintained a website for many of the courses I taught. For courses, such as CSCB09 that I TA'ed multiple times, I would take on additional responsibilities (helping the instructor with office hours, assisting other TA's with their questions and marking, working through technical issues with the system administrator). I have also volunteered my time to mentor the next generation of incoming TA's.
